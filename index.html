Usage

Define your settings in the right hand panel. Higher values would give bigger worlds but also slower generation.

Place your cursor in the center of the browser and press "Q" for free-look.

Movement:

  • Mouse - Look around.
  • "Q" - Pause / Resume (press before using controls)
  • "W, A, S, D" - Fly controls
  • Left Click - Forward
  • Right Click - Backward
  • "SPACE" - Throw TNT (experimental - use only in chunks less than 64)
